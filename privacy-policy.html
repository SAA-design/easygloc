Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

Introduction

At EasyGluco, we prioritize your privacy by design. Our app operates entirely on your device, with no data collection or external server communication.

Local Data Storage

All your data, including:

  • Blood glucose readings
  • HbA1c calculations
  • Personal settings
  • Reminder preferences

is only stored on your device and never leaves your device.

No Data Collection

We do not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Track your usage
  • Use analytics services
  • Store data on external servers
  • Share any information with third parties

Apple Health Integration

If you choose to use Apple Health integration, this interaction is handled directly by your device's operating system. We do not receive or process any of this data.

Your Data Control

You have complete control over your data. You can:

  • Delete all app data at any time
  • Export your data for personal use
  • Manage app permissions through your device settings
